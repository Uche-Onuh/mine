Our syllabus is positioned to give you tools and resources to help you grow you skills. The fashion business is ever changing and the learning process never stops. We equip you with all the materials to keep you ahead of the curve.

Training materials

  • Tape rule
  • Tracing wheel
  • French curve
  • Hip curve
  • Chalk
  • Pencils
  • Two scissors (paper scissors and cutting scissors)
  • One roll of Paper (white or brown)
  • Armhole curve
  • 3-6 yards Calico/materia
  • One pack of manual machine needle
  • One manual machine zipper foot and sewing foot
  • Hand stitching needle
  • Sketch pad
  • 24 set Crayola coloring pencils
  • Sketching pencil: RotringTikky0.5
