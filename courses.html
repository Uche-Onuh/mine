Pattern Drafting

Create clothing patterns with great fit and unique styles. Nothing beats the look of custom crafted clothings. Patterns you make yourself will show off your own unique style and personality and let you stand out in the crowd. You can then use your block to make your own designs, adding style lines, flare, collars, pockets and details to create whatever new styles you like. You can depend on your block to deliver every time – the fit is already built in so you won’t be wasting time adjusting sleeves, bust darts, or waistlines on every garment you make. If you spend the time and effort now to create a perfectly fitted block, you’ll save yourself time and frustration later! Your clothing patterns will fit perfectly every time.
